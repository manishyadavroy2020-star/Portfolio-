FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my website?+

Basic websites are delivered in 3 days, Standard in 5 days and Premium in 7 days. I work fast without compromising on quality. Once you share your business details and content, I get started immediately.

What if I don't have a logo?+

No problem at all! Free logo design is included with every package. Just tell me your business name and what you do — I'll design a professional logo that matches your brand perfectly at zero extra cost.

Will my website work on mobile phones?+

Absolutely! Every website I build is 100% mobile responsive and tested on all screen sizes. Since most customers browse on phones, mobile optimization is my top priority on every project.

Can I update my website later?+

Yes! Small updates like changing phone numbers, prices or text are done for free. For bigger changes or new sections, I charge a small fee. I'm always available on WhatsApp — just message me anytime.

What do I need to provide you?+

Just your business name, what you do, your contact details (phone, WhatsApp, address) and any photos you have. If you don't have photos, I'll use professional stock images. That's all I need to get started!
