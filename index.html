FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my website?+

Basic websites are delivered in 3 days, Standard in 5 days and Premium in 7 days. Once you share your business details, I get started immediately.

What if I don't have a logo?+

No problem! Free logo design is included with every package. Just tell me your business name — I'll design a professional logo at zero extra cost.

Will my website work on mobile phones?+

Absolutely! Every website I build is 100% mobile responsive and tested on all screen sizes. Mobile optimization is my top priority.

Can I update my website later?+

Yes! Small updates like changing phone numbers or prices are done free. I'm always available on WhatsApp — just message me anytime.

What do I need to provide you?+

Just your business name, what you do, contact details and any photos. If you don't have photos, I'll use professional stock images. That's all!
